## The 30-second pitch

**Turn your tanker into steady income.** KitUm sends water-delivery customers straight to your phone — no hunting for work, no middlemen. You keep **95% of every fare**, choose your own hours, and get paid for every trip. **Joining is free.**

## 1. Getting started

**Does it cost anything to join?**
No. Registration is **completely free** — no joining fee, no deposit, no monthly charge. You only ever pay KitUm a small commission on deliveries you actually complete.

**What do I need to register?**
- Your phone number and an email
- Your **vehicle/tanker number** and its **capacity** (litres)
- A quick **selfie** (taken in the app with your camera)
- **PAN card** and **driver's licence** (front & back) for verification
- **Bank account or UPI** to receive your earnings

**How long does approval take?**
Once you submit your documents, our team verifies them and activates your account. You'll see your status in the app the whole time. **[confirm: typical turnaround — e.g. "usually within 24–48 hours"]**

**Can I sign up if I already drive for someone else?**
Yes. KitUm is flexible — work it alongside whatever else you do.

**How much will I earn?**
You keep **95% of the delivery fare** — KitUm's commission is just **5%**. The fare for each tanker size is shown to you *before* you accept, so you always know exactly what you'll earn.

## 3. Getting & doing deliveries

**How do I receive orders?**
Flip the **"Go online"** switch and KitUm sends nearby delivery requests straight to your phone — with a **loud alarm**, even when the app is in your pocket. Accept the ones you want.

**Will I miss requests if my phone is locked?**
No — as long as notifications are on, an incoming request **rings like a call** even with the app closed or the screen off. (Enable notifications + location when the app asks — that's how we reach you.)

**Do I have to accept every request?**
No. Each offer gives you about **20 seconds** to Accept or Decline, with **no penalty** for declining. (Naturally, the more you accept, the more you earn.)

**Can I choose my own hours?**
Completely. Go online whenever you want, go offline anytime — **no shifts, no targets, no penalty** for taking a break or a day off.

**How do I find the customer?**
The app gives you **one-tap navigation** to the drop-off and lets you **call or WhatsApp** the customer directly. You see the address, tanker size, distance, and your earning up front.

**What areas does KitUm serve?**
Shillong and the surrounding serviceable areas. You'll receive requests near **your location**, so you're never sent far out of the way. **[confirm: list serviced localities/zones.]**

## 4. You're protected

**What if the customer isn't there when I arrive?**
You're covered. If a customer is unreachable at the drop-off, **call them from the app and wait a few minutes**; if they still don't show, report a no-show and you **still get paid a trip fee (₹50)** for your time and fuel — you don't go home empty-handed.

**What if a customer keeps cancelling on me?**
KitUm tracks this. Customers who repeatedly cancel **after a partner is assigned** get restricted from booking — your time is respected.

**What if I can't complete a delivery (breakdown, etc.)?**
You can **release the job** in one tap and we'll reassign it to another partner — no penalty for genuine issues. Help & support is always in the app.

**What about my rating?**
Customers rate each delivery. Good service keeps you **top-rated and getting more requests**. One stray low rating won't sink you — your score is an **average over many trips**, so consistency is what matters.

**Is my information safe?**
Yes. Your PAN, licence and photo are stored **privately and securely** and used only for verification — never shown to customers.

## 5. Account & support

**Can I change my vehicle or details later?**
Yes — raise a quick request in the app's **Help & Support** and our team will update it.

**What if I have a problem during a delivery or with a payment?**
**Help & Support** is built into the app — message us anytime and we'll sort it out.

**What happens if a customer disputes a delivery or wants a refund?**
KitUm handles customer disputes and refunds on our side — you focus on delivering. Genuine completed deliveries are always paid.
